    • CareerPulse: An AI-Powered Career Intelligence Platform

    CareerPulse is a full-stack career intelligence platform designed to help users understand hiring trends, identify in-demand skills, analyze resumes, discover visa-friendly companies, and receive personalized AI-powered career recommendations.

    The platform combines live job market data, career news, resume analysis, and AI-generated guidance to help users make smarter, data-driven career decisions.

    🎯 Objectives

    • 📊 Analyze job market trends and skill demand.
    • 🤖 Provide AI-powered career recommendations.
    • 📄 Identify resume skill gaps and improvement areas.
    • 🌎 Highlight visa-friendly companies and hiring opportunities.
    • 📰 Display career market news and industry updates.
    • 🎨 Build a polished, modern dashboard-style user experience.

    🛠️ Technologies Used

    🖥️ Frontend Development

    • ⚛️ React.js – Component-based frontend architecture.
    • ⚡ Vite – Fast development and build tooling.
    • 📡 Axios – Handles frontend API requests.
    • 📈 Recharts – Interactive analytics visualizations.
    • 🎨 CSS3 – Responsive custom UI styling.

    🌍 Backend & APIs

    • 🟢 Node.js – Backend runtime environment.
    • 🚂 Express.js – REST API backend server.
    • 🤖 OpenAI API – AI career advisor and recommendations.
    • 🔍 JSearch API – Live job market data through RapidAPI.
    • 📰 NewsAPI – Career market and technology news.
    • 🔐 JWT – Authentication and session handling.

    📊 Data Engineering & DevOps

    • 🐘 PostgreSQL – Relational database schema design.
    • 🐍 Python – ETL pipeline starter logic.
    • 🌬️ Apache Airflow – Pipeline orchestration structure.
    • 📦 dbt – Analytics transformation models.
    • 🐳 Docker – Containerized application setup.
    • 🔄 GitHub Actions – CI/CD workflow configuration.

    📂 Project Structure

    🖥️ Frontend Pages & Components

    • 🏠 Home Page – Overview metrics and navigation cards.
    • 📈 Market Insights – Skill demand, salary, location, and industry analytics.
    • 📄 Resume Analyzer – Resume parsing and skill gap analysis.
    • 🤖 AI Career Advisor – Personalized AI-generated career guidance.
    • 🌎 Visa Watchlist – Visa-friendly company and role discovery.
    • 📰 Career News – Technology hiring and market news.

    🖧 Backend Services

    • 📊 analyticsService.js – Calculates trends, distributions, and market summaries.
    • 🔍 jobDataService.js – Fetches and normalizes live job market data.
    • 🤖 advisorService.js – Sends career context to OpenAI for recommendations.
    • 📄 resumeService.js – Extracts skills from resume text.
    • 📰 newsService.js – Fetches career and technology news.
    • ⚙️ server.js – Express server configuration.

    🚀 Features & Functionality

    • 📊 Market Analytics Dashboard – Tracks hiring trends, skill demand, salaries, locations, and industries.
    • 📈 Skill Demand Index – Identifies the most requested skills across job postings.
    • ⚡ Skill Momentum Index – Compares current skill trends with prior market data.
    • 📄 Resume Gap Analysis – Compares user skills against market demand.
    • 🤖 AI Career Advisor – Generates personalized career recommendations.
    • 🌎 Visa-Friendly Company Watchlist – Detects companies and roles that may support international candidates.
    • 📰 Career Market News – Displays recent news affecting technology hiring and career trends.
