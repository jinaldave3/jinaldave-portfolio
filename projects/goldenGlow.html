    • Golden Glow Connect: A Web-Based Support Platform for Menopausal Health

    Masters Project – New Jersey Institute of Technology


    Golden Glow Connect is an interactive web platform designed to provide a supportive community for individuals seeking information, discussions, and expert guidance on menopausal health and wellness topics. It integrates a news feed, community discussions, a MenoAdvisor chatbot, and user authentication to foster engagement and accessibility.


    Menopause is a natural biological transition that affects millions of women globally. However, the experience is often isolating, with social stigma, lack of awareness, and limited support contributing to distress. Golden Glow Connect was developed to bridge this gap by leveraging technology to provide a safe space for discussions, information sharing, and AI-driven guidance.

    Technologies Used

    • 📄 HTML5 – Structuring content and layout.
    • 🎨 CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid) – Styling and responsive design.
    • 🟨 JavaScript (ES6+) – Interactive components and dynamic content rendering.
    • 🔤 Google Fonts & SVG Icons – Enhanced readability and UI elements.
    • 🔥 Firebase Authentication – Secure user login and account management.
    • 📂 Firebase Realtime Database – Storing discussions, user profiles, and chatbot interactions.
    • 🤖 MenoAdvisor Chatbot (AI-based) – Provides automated answers to menopause-related queries.

    Project Structure

    1️⃣ User Authentication (Login & Signup)

    Secure user registration and login using Firebase Authentication.

    2️⃣ News Feed (Educational Content)

    A curated feed of articles on menopause, wellness, and aging.

    3️⃣ Community Discussions

    A forum-style space for discussions, Q&A, and expert interactions.

    4️⃣ MenoAdvisor Chatbot

    A virtual assistant providing AI-powered responses to menopause-related queries.

    Limitations & Challenges

    🚫 No Medical Diagnosis – The chatbot and forum do not replace professional medical advice.
    🛑 Moderation Scope – The platform relies on user-reported content violations.
    ⚠️ User-Generated Content Risks – Misinformation may be shared in discussions.

    Future Enhancements

    • Multilingual Support – Automatic translation of discussions.
    • Private Support Groups – Focused discussions for specific health concerns.
    • Wellness Space – Mood tracking, guided meditations, symptom logs.
    • AI-Powered Content Recommendations – Smart suggestions for articles and discussions.

    Golden Glow Connect represents a technological approach to addressing menopause-related challenges. By integrating AI-powered chat assistance, a structured forum, and curated wellness content, it creates a safe, supportive, and informative digital space for individuals navigating menopause.
